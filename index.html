How it works. Slopguard looks for the tells of AI’s default writing voice — stock phrases (“leverage”, “seamless”, “delve”), the “it’s not just X — it’s Y” move, em-dash drama, and rule-of-three lists. It’s good at catching text written in that default voice. It can’t catch an AI that’s deliberately imitating one specific person’s style — and the cleaner version is a quick fix, not a full rewrite (use the prompt for that).
